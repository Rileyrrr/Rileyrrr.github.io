Focusing on Web Design

  • What is the company/organization?
  • Cable News Network
  • What is the URL?
  • CNN.com
  • What is the purpose of the site?
  • The purpose of the site is to inform people on the latest news and information about a variety of topics.
  • Who is the intended audience?
  • The intended audience are educated people that want to learn more information about the United States and the world.
  • Do you believe the site reaches its audience?
  • I do believe that the site reaches its intended audience because it is the most well-known source of news with credidible stories.
  • Was the site useful to you? Why or why not?
  • This site was very useful to me because I am able to learn new things that others may not be aware of.
